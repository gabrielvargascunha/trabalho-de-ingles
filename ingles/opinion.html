    • MY Opinion

    “Explaining Money” is a Netflix series that is part of the "Explained" collection. With short and informative episodes,
    the series demystifies complex concepts about economics and finance, making them accessible to the general public.
    It covers a broad range of topics, from the history of money to cryptocurrencies, inflation, and wealth inequality.
    Episodes are visually appealing, using graphics and animations to illustrate important points,and include interviews with experts,
    presenting different perspectives on the topics discussed.With an average duration of 20 to 30 minutes per episode,
    the series is ideal for those who want to learn about finance quickly and interestingly.
    In summary, "Explaining Money" is an excellent option for anyone who wants to better understand the world of finance
    and current economic trends, all presented in a clear and engaging way.
