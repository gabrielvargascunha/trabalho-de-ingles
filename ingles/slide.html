    • MONEY

    Este episódio introdutório explora o conceito de dinheiro, desde suas origens até seu papel na sociedade moderna. Ele aborda questões como a história do dinheiro, como ele é criado pelos bancos centrais, e como funciona o sistema bancário atual..

    Cryptocurrencies

    This episode deals with cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum, exploring their emergence, how they function, and their potential impact on the traditional financial system. It covers topics such as blockchain, cryptocurrency mining, and their role as an alternative to traditional money.

    Income Inequality

    In this episode, the series examines income inequality, exploring its causes and consequences. It discusses how income inequality affects society and the role that government policies can play in reducing this disparity

    Market Economy

    This episode explores how the market economy works, highlighting its fundamental principles and how it influences people's lives worldwide. It also addresses issues such as competition, supply and demand, and how governments intervene in the economy.

    Stock Market

    In this episode, the series investigates how the stock market works, explaining how companies are listed, how stocks are traded, and how investors can profit from the stock market.

    Credit and Cards

    This episode covers credit and credit cards, exploring how they work, the risks associated with debt, and how credit card companies profit

    Financial Frauds

    In this episode, the series explores different types of financial fraud, from pyramid schemes to bank fraud, and how consumers can protect themselves against them.

    Taxes

    This episode discusses the tax system, explaining how income, consumption, and property taxes work, and how they are used to finance public services.
